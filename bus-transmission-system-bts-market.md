<p><p>The Bus Transmission System (BTS) encompasses the mechanisms that facilitate the transfer of power from the engine to the wheels in buses. It primarily includes two market types: Manual Transmission Systems and Automatic Transmission Systems. Manual transmissions require drivers to shift gears manually, offering greater control and efficiency, particularly in varied driving conditions. In contrast, automatic transmissions simplify driving by automatically adjusting gears, enhancing convenience and comfort, especially for urban environments and heavy traffic situations.</p></p>
