<p><p>The UV LED market is segmented into three main types: UV-A, UV-B, and UV-C LEDs. UV-A LEDs, operating between 320-400 nm, are commonly used in applications like curing and detecting counterfeit materials. UV-B LEDs, ranging from 280-320 nm, are primarily employed in therapeutic and horticultural applications. UV-C LEDs, with wavelengths below 280 nm, are crucial for disinfection and sterilization, particularly in healthcare and water treatment sectors. Each type serves distinct applications, driving demand across various industries.</p></p>
