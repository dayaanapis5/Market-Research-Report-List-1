<p><p>The breast cancer testing market comprises three main types: screening tests, diagnostic tests, and monitoring tests. Screening tests, such as mammograms and clinical breast exams, aim to detect cancer early in asymptomatic individuals. Diagnostic tests, including biopsies and imaging studies, confirm the presence and type of cancer in symptomatic patients. Monitoring tests are used to track the disease's progression or recurrence after treatment, ensuring effective management and timely interventions based on the patient's needs and condition.</p></p>
