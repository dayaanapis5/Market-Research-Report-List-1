<p><p>The Intelligent Radar Level Gauge market is divided into two primary types: High Frequency Radar Level Gauges and Low Frequency Radar Level Gauges. High Frequency Radar Gauges, operating typically above 6 GHz, offer precise measurements in small and complex vessels, suitable for detecting low dielectric constant materials. In contrast, Low Frequency Radar Gauges operate generally below 6 GHz, making them ideal for large tanks or harsh conditions, effectively measuring materials with varying densities and permit greater signal penetration through obstacles like vapors and foam.</p></p>
