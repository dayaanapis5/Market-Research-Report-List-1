<p><p>The Strontium Oxide Sputtering Target Market is categorized into two main types: Rotatable and Non-Rotating targets. Rotatable targets are designed to allow continuous rotation during the sputtering process, enhancing uniformity and efficiency in film deposition. They are ideal for large-area coatings and high-throughput applications. In contrast, Non-Rotating targets are stationary and typically used for smaller-scale applications. They can be more cost-effective for limited production needs but may require more frequent target replacements due to uneven wear.</p></p>
