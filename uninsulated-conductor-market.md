<p><p>The uninsulated conductor market can be categorized into two main types: single wire type and multi-wire type. Single wire conductors consist of a single strand, offering simplicity and ease of installation, often used in applications requiring flexibility and straightforward connections. In contrast, multi-wire conductors are composed of multiple strands bundled together, providing enhanced conductivity and resistance to mechanical stress. This type is preferred in high-current applications and environments where durability and flexibility are essential, making them suitable for various industrial uses.</p></p>
