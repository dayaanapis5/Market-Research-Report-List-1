<p><p>The Multiple Sclerosis (MS) treatment market features three primary drug types: Interferons, Immunomodulators, and Immunosuppressants. Interferons are proteins that modify immune responses, reducing relapse rates. Immunomodulators, such as Glatiramer acetate, enhance immune regulation and offer alternative treatment options. Immunosuppressants work by directly inhibiting immune system activity, effectively managing severe MS cases. Each type offers unique mechanisms and benefits, catering to different patient needs and disease progression, thus impacting treatment choices and market dynamics significantly.</p></p>
