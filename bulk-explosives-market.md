<p><strong>The Bulk Explosives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ammonium Nitrate Explosives (Powder)</li><li>ANFO</li><li>Emulsion Explosive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bulk explosives market is primarily characterized by three types: Ammonium Nitrate Explosives (ANE), ANFO (Ammonium Nitrate Fuel Oil), and Emulsion Explosives. ANE is a powdered explosive that offers high energy and is used in various applications. ANFO combines ammonium nitrate and fuel oil for cost-effective blasting, widely used in mining and construction. Emulsion explosives are water-resistant mixtures suitable for wet conditions, allowing for efficient detonation in challenging environments. Each type serves specific industry needs effectively.</p></p>
