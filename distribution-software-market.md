<p><ul><li>Cloudbased</li><li>On-premises</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Distribution software encompasses two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based distribution software is hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access the system via the internet. This model offers scalability, reduced upfront costs, and easier updates. In contrast, on-premises distribution software is installed locally on a company's servers, providing greater control over data and security. However, it typically requires significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance, making it suitable for organizations with specific compliance or customization needs.</p></p>
