<p><p>Medical Andersen Cascade Impactors (ACI) are devices used to measure the aerodynamic particle size distribution of inhalable aerosols, crucial for assessing inhalation therapies. The Compact Cascade Impactor (CCI) Five Stage features five collection stages, allowing for effective size separation and analysis of particles. Both models are integral in pharmaceutical research for optimizing aerosol delivery systems and ensuring therapeutic efficacy.</p></p>
