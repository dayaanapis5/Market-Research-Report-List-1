<p><p>The functional whey protein concentrate market includes three main types: Whey Protein Concentrate (WPC), which retains beneficial nutrients but contains higher fat and lactose levels; Whey Protein Isolate (WPI), which is processed to remove most fats and lactose, making it purer; and Hydrolyzed Whey Protein (HWP), which undergoes partial hydrolysis for easier digestion and faster absorption. Each type serves different consumer needs, from general nutritional support to specialized dietary requirements and athletic performance enhancement.</p></p>
