<p><p>The hospital autoclaves market encompasses three main types: manual, fully automatic, and semi-automatic autoclaves. Manual hospital autoclaves require the operator to control all processes, making them cost-effective but labor-intensive. Fully automatic hospital autoclaves streamline operations with automated cycles, ensuring consistent sterilization with minimal user intervention, ideal for busy settings. Semi-automatic hospital autoclaves offer a balance, automating certain processes while still requiring user input, catering to facilities seeking efficiency without full automation. Each type serves distinct operational needs in healthcare facilities.</p></p>
