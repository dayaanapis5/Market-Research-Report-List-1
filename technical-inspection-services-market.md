<p><strong>The Technical Inspection Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional NDT</li><li>Advanced NDT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Technical Inspection Services market comprises two main types: Conventional Nondestructive Testing (NDT) and Advanced NDT. Conventional NDT includes traditional methods such as visual inspection, ultrasonic testing, and radiography, primarily focusing on detecting surface and subsurface defects. In contrast, Advanced NDT employs sophisticated techniques like phased array ultrasonics, digital radiography, and computed tomography, allowing for more comprehensive and precise evaluations. Both segments play crucial roles in ensuring safety, integrity, and reliability across various industries, including manufacturing, construction, and aerospace.</p></p>
