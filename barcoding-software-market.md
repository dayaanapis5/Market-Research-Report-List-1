<p><p>The barcoding software market is categorized into three types: one-dimensional (1D) barcoding, two-dimensional (2D) barcoding, and 3D barcoding. One-dimensional barcoding uses linear patterns, widely utilized in retail and logistics for simple tracking. Two-dimensional barcoding encodes more data in compact formats, popular in ticketing and inventory management. Emerging 3D barcoding adds depth perception, enabling enhanced data storage and scanning efficiency, suitable for complex applications in industries like healthcare and manufacturing where detailed information is essential.</p></p>
