<p><strong>The Infant Clinical Nutrition Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oral administration</li><li>Enteral administration</li><li>Intravenous administration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Infant Clinical Nutrition Market encompasses three primary types of administration. Oral administration involves providing nutritional products directly through the mouth, typically in the form of specialized formulas for infants. Enteral administration delivers nutrients via a feeding tube directly into the gastrointestinal tract, ideal for infants who cannot feed orally. Intravenous administration involves delivering nutrients directly into the bloodstream, ensuring rapid absorption, and is often used for critically ill infants or those with severe feeding challenges.</p></p>
