<p><ul><li>Fully Threaded Bolt</li><li>Partially Threaded Bolt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The small diameter bolt market can be divided into two main types: fully threaded bolts and partially threaded bolts. Fully threaded bolts feature threads that extend along the entire length, providing uniform strength and ensuring a secure fit in applications requiring complete engagement. In contrast, partially threaded bolts have threads confined to a portion of the shaft, allowing for better alignment and flexibility in certain fastening scenarios. Both types serve various industries, including automotive, construction, and manufacturing, catering to specific structural needs.</p></p>
