<p><p>FIPS market types for de-icing and anti-icing systems focus on technologies and products used to manage ice and snow on surfaces such as roads, runways, and transportation infrastructures. De-icing systems aim to remove existing ice, employing chemicals or mechanical methods to ensure safety during adverse weather conditions.
