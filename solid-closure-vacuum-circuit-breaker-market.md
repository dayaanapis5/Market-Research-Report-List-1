<p><p>The Solid-closure Vacuum Circuit Breaker (VCB) market encompasses two main types: Medium Voltage and High Voltage VCBs. Medium Voltage VCBs typically operate in voltage ranges from 1 kV to 35 kV, offering safety and reliability for industrial and commercial applications.
