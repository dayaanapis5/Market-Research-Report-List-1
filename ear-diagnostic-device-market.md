<p><ul><li>OAE Machines</li><li>Audiometer</li><li>Otoscope</li><li>Tympanometer</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ear diagnostic device market encompasses several types of equipment used for assessing hearing and ear health. Otoacoustic Emissions (OAE) machines evaluate cochlear function by measuring sound waves produced in the inner ear. Audiometers determine hearing thresholds across various frequencies. Otoscopes allow visual examination of the ear canal and eardrum. Tympanometers assess middle ear function by measuring eardrum mobility. Other devices in the market may include impedance meters and handheld screening tools, contributing to comprehensive ear diagnostics.</p></p>
