<p><p>The tablet processing equipment market encompasses various types essential for pharmaceutical manufacturing. Vibrio sifters are used for particle size separation, ensuring uniformity in formulations. Granulators mix powders and facilitate granule formation. Tablet presses compress powders into tablets with specified shapes and sizes. Dryers remove moisture to enhance stability, while tablet coating machines apply films for protection and aesthetic enhancement. Additional types, referred to as “others,” may include equipment like magnetic separators and packaging machines, further supporting production processes.</p></p>
