<p><strong>The Oral Dissolvable Films Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oral Dispersible</li><li>Sublingual</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oral dissolvable films market comprises two primary types: oral dispersible films and sublingual films. Oral dispersible films dissolve in the mouth, releasing active ingredients that can be swallowed or absorbed through the oral mucosa, appealing for those who have difficulty swallowing pills. Sublingual films, on the other hand, are placed under the tongue, allowing for rapid absorption directly into the bloodstream. Both types offer convenience and improved patient compliance, catering to diverse therapeutic needs in medication administration.</p></p>
