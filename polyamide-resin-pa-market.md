<p><strong>The Polyamide Resin (PA) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reactive Type</li><li>Nonreactive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyamide resin (PA) comes in two main market types: reactive and nonreactive. Reactive polyamide resins undergo chemical changes when cured, allowing for strong bonds and durability, making them suitable for adhesives and coatings. Nonreactive polyamide resins, on the other hand, remain stable and do not undergo curing, making them ideal for applications that require flexibility and resistance to chemicals. The choice between these types depends on the specific performance needs in industrial applications, such as automotive, construction, and consumer products.</p></p>
