<p><p>The 3D Coordinate Measuring Machines (CMM) market encompasses two main types: non-contact and contact. Non-contact CMMs utilize optical or laser technologies to measure parts without physical contact, making them ideal for delicate or soft materials. Conversely, contact CMMs use probing devices that physically touch the object to capture measurements, offering high precision and reliability for rigid components. Both types serve diverse industries, including automotive and aerospace, by ensuring accurate dimensions and quality control in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
