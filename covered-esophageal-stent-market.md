<p><p>The covered esophageal stent market is categorized into two main types: fully covered and partially covered stents. Fully covered esophageal stents are designed with a complete layer of covering material, providing optimal protection against tissue ingrowth and allowing for easy removal. In contrast, partially covered stents feature a combination of covered and uncovered sections, facilitating tissue integration while still preventing significant obstruction. Both types serve to manage strictures and other esophageal conditions, with specific applications based on clinical requirements.</p></p>
