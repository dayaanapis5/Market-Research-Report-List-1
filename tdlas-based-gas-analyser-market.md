<p><p>The TDLAS (Tunable Diode Laser Absorption Spectroscopy) gas analyser market is divided into two main types: In-Situ and Extractive TDLAS based analysers. In-Situ TDLAS analysers measure gas concentrations directly in the environment, providing real-time data without sample handling, ideal for continuous monitoring in harsh conditions. Extractive TDLAS analysers, on the other hand, involve collecting gas samples and analyzing them externally. This method is suitable for laboratory settings and can offer higher sensitivity and precision in measurements.</p></p>
