<p><ul><li>Cardiopulmonary Exercise Testing (CPET) Systems</li><li>Stress ECG</li><li>Pulse Oximeters</li><li>Stress Blood Pressure Monitors</li><li>Single-Photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cardiopulmonary stress testing systems market comprises several types, each serving unique diagnostic purposes. Cardiopulmonary Exercise Testing (CPET) systems assess cardiovascular and pulmonary response to exercise. Stress ECG monitors heart activity during physical exertion, detecting arrhythmias. Pulse oximeters measure blood oxygen levels, providing insights into respiratory efficiency. Stress blood pressure monitors track hemodynamic responses under stress. Lastly, Single-Photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT) offers detailed imaging for cardiac perfusion evaluation, crucial for assessing coronary artery diseases and other heart conditions.</p></p>
