<p><p>Video on Demand (VOD) services encompass three main market types: Transactional Video on Demand (TVOD), Subscription Video on Demand (SVOD), and Near Video on Demand (NVOD). TVOD allows users to pay for individual titles, ideal for occasional viewers. SVOD offers unlimited access to a library for a monthly fee, appealing to binge-watchers. NVOD provides a scheduled selection of content available for viewing at specific times, blending traditional broadcasting with VOD convenience, catering to diverse consumer preferences in content consumption.</p></p>
