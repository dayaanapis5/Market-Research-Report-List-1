<p><strong>The Copper-Aluminum Clad Metals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inlay</li><li>Overlay</li><li>Edgelay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Copper-Aluminum Clad Metals market includes three main types: inlay, overlay, and edgelay. Inlay involves embedding copper within aluminum substrates, enhancing conductivity while maintaining lightweight properties. Overlay features a layer of copper bonded to the surface of aluminum, providing improved electrical and thermal performance. Edgelay, on the other hand, focuses on cladding the edges of aluminum components with copper, ensuring effective conductivity and strength at critical junctions. Each type offers unique benefits tailored to specific applications in electronics and engineering.</p></p>
