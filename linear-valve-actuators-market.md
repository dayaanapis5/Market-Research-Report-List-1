<p><p>Linear valve actuators are essential devices used to control valve movement in various applications. The diaphragm actuator employs a flexible diaphragm to regulate fluid flow, offering precise control and quick response. It’s suitable for low-pressure systems. Conversely, piston actuators utilize a piston mechanism to generate linear motion, providing higher force output and better performance in heavy-duty applications. Both types serve different operational needs, emphasizing reliability and efficiency in industries such as oil and gas, water treatment, and manufacturing.</p></p>
