<p><p>The turret lathe market can be categorized into four main types: manual, semi-automatic, automatic, and CNC. Semi-automatic lathes provide some automated processes, reducing labor while still needing operator input. Automatic turret lathes operate with minimal human intervention, enhancing efficiency and consistency.
