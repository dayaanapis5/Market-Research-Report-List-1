<p><ul><li>Seamless Tubing</li><li>Spiral Wound Tubing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyimide tube market is categorized into seamless tubing and spiral wound tubing. Seamless tubing is characterized by its uniform structure, offering high strength and thermal stability, making it ideal for applications in aerospace and electronics. Spiral wound tubing consists of layers wound in a helical pattern, providing flexibility and enhanced durability. This type is often used in flexible applications where bending and twisting are required. Both types are prized for their high-performance properties in demanding environments.</p></p>
