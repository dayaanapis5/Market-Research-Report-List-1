<p><strong>The Wireless Electric Vehicle Charger Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Magnetic Resonance Technology</li><li>Inductive Charging Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless electric vehicle (EV) charger market comprises two primary technologies: magnetic resonance and inductive charging. Magnetic resonance technology uses oscillating magnetic fields to transfer energy between coils at a distance, allowing for more flexibility in alignment between the charger and vehicle. Inductive charging, on the other hand, relies on close proximity between two coils, transferring energy through direct electromagnetic induction. Both technologies aim to enhance the convenience and efficiency of EV charging, reducing the reliance on traditional plug-in methods.</p></p>
