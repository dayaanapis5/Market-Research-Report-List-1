<p><p>The die grinder market is primarily categorized into pneumatic and hydraulic types. Pneumatic die grinders are powered by compressed air, offering high-speed performance and versatility for various materials, making them ideal for industrial applications. In contrast, hydraulic die grinders utilize hydraulic fluid for power, providing stronger torque and better control in demanding environments. Both types cater to different user needs, with pneumatic models favored for portability and ease of use, while hydraulic grinders excel in heavy-duty tasks.</p></p>
