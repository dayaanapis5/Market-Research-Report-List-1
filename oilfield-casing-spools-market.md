<p><ul><li>Light Casing String Spool</li><li>Intermediate Casing String Spool</li><li>Extreme Casing String Spool</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oilfield casing spools market includes three main types: Light Casing String Spool, Intermediate Casing String Spool, and Extreme Casing String Spool. Light casing spools are designed for shallow wells with lighter loads, while intermediate spools cater to medium-depth wells, providing a robust structure to support varied pressure levels. Extreme casing spools are engineered for deep wells, optimized for high-pressure environments and demanding geological conditions, ensuring the integrity and safety of the wellbore throughout drilling operations.</p></p>
