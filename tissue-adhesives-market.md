<p><p>The tissue adhesives market encompasses various types, each serving specific medical applications. Protein-based adhesives utilize natural proteins for bonding tissues, while collagen-based adhesives leverage collagen's biocompatibility to promote healing. Thrombin-based adhesives facilitate rapid clotting, essential in surgical settings. Fibrin adhesives mimic the final stages of blood coagulation, providing effective wound closure. Other adhesives may include synthetic options or innovative biomaterials, enhancing versatility in surgical procedures. Together, these adhesives improve patient outcomes and reduce healing time.</p></p>
