<p><p>The three-phase string inverter market comprises two primary types: on-grid and off-grid string inverters. On-grid string inverters connect solar power systems to the utility grid, allowing for energy export and net metering, enhancing energy efficiency. In contrast, off-grid string inverters operate independently from the grid, providing electricity directly to local loads or battery storage systems, often in remote areas. Both types are essential for optimizing solar energy generation and usage based on specific user needs and energy infrastructures.</p></p>
