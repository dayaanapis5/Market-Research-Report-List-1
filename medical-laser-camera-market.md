<p><ul><li>Dry Type</li><li>Wet Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical laser camera market is divided into two main types: dry type and wet type. Dry type cameras utilize a digital imaging system that captures and processes images without the need for liquid solutions, making them faster and more convenient for quick diagnostics. In contrast, wet type cameras require the use of chemical solutions for developing images, often providing higher-quality output but with a more elongated process. Both types cater to different medical imaging needs and workflows in healthcare settings.</p></p>
