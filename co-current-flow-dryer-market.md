<p><p>A co-current flow dryer is a type of industrial dryer where the air or drying gas flows in the same direction as the material being dried. This mechanism enhances the efficiency of heat transfer and moisture removal, making it ideal for products sensitive to high temperatures. Commonly used in food processing, pharmaceuticals, and chemical industries, co-current flow drying systems help maintain product quality by minimizing oxidation and thermal degradation.</p><p>The Co-Current Flow Dryer Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for efficient drying solutions across various sectors.
