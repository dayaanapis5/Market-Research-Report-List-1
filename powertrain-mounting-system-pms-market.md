<p><p>The Powertrain Mounting System (PMS) market comprises various types, primarily focusing on Pure Rubber Mounting and Hydraulic Mounting systems. Pure Rubber Mountings are designed to absorb engine vibrations and provide basic isolation, making them cost-effective and reliable for various vehicles. In contrast, Hydraulic Mountings utilize fluid-filled compartments to offer superior vibration dampening and noise reduction, enhancing ride comfort and performance. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing vehicle dynamics and improving overall driving experience.</p></p>
