<p><p>The Plasma Surface Treatment Equipment market is divided into two primary types: Low Pressure/Vacuum Plasma Surface Treatment Equipment and Atmospheric Plasma Surface Treatment Equipment. Low pressure or vacuum systems operate in controlled environments, creating high-quality surface modifications for materials like plastics and metals, enhancing adhesion and wettability. In contrast, atmospheric plasma systems operate at ambient pressure, offering quicker processing times and versatility for inline applications, making them ideal for surface cleaning, activation, or coating in various industries.</p></p>
