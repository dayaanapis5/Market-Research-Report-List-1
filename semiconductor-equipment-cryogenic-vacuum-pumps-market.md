<p><p>The Semiconductor Equipment Cryogenic Vacuum Pumps market is primarily divided into two types: water-cooled cryopumps and air-cooled cryopumps. Water-cooled cryopumps utilize a water circulation system to maintain low temperatures, achieving higher pumping speeds and efficiency in removing gases and contaminants. In contrast, air-cooled cryopumps rely on ambient air for cooling, making them more suitable for smaller applications or environments where water usage is restricted. Both types are essential for maintaining high vacuum conditions in semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
