<p><p>The small hydro turbine market includes several key types: Francis, Kaplan, Pelton, and others. Francis turbines are suited for medium head and flow conditions, making them versatile for various sites. Kaplan turbines operate efficiently at low heads with high flow rates, ideal for river applications. Pelton turbines excel in high head, low flow scenarios, harnessing kinetic energy from water jets. Other turbine types encompass diverse designs catering to specific geographical and operational conditions, enhancing overall energy generation efficiency.</p></p>
