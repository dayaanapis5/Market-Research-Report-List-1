<p><p>The oxygen therapy consumables market includes various devices designed to deliver supplemental oxygen. The Venturi mask provides precise oxygen concentrations using color-coded adapters, ideal for controlled therapy. Nasal cannulas are lightweight tubes inserted into the nostrils for low-flow oxygen delivery, favored for comfort. CPAP masks deliver continuous positive airway pressure to aid in sleep apnea treatment, ensuring airway stability during sleep. Simple oxygen masks cover the nose and mouth, providing moderate oxygen flow for general use in medical settings.</p></p>
