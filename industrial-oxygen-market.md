<p><p>The industrial oxygen market consists of three main types: On-Site, Bulk, and Cylinder. </p><p>On-Site Type involves generating oxygen directly at the facility using specialized equipment, ensuring continuous supply and reduced transportation costs. </p><p>Bulk Type refers to large-scale delivery of oxygen in liquid form, stored in cryogenic tanks for significant industrial operations requiring high volumes. </p><p>Cylinder Type consists of oxygen gas stored in high-pressure cylinders, suitable for smaller-scale applications and mobility, commonly used in medical, welding, and laboratory settings.</p></p>
