<p><p>The particle board adhesives market is segmented into solvent-based, water-based, and solventless types. Solvent-based adhesives contain organic solvents, offering strong bonding but raising environmental concerns. Water-based adhesives are eco-friendly, utilizing water as a solvent, making them safer and less harmful to human health. Solventless adhesives eliminate solvents entirely, providing a sustainable option that minimizes emissions and fosters a safer working environment. Each type caters to specific industry needs, balancing performance, safety, and environmental impact.</p></p>
