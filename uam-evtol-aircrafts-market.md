Vector propulsion systems utilize tilting rotors or fans to achieve directional control, enhancing maneuverability. Vectorless propulsion relies on fixed rotor configurations, typically optimizing for efficiency and simplicity in design. Other types may include hybrid systems or innovative propulsion methods that do not fit neatly into the previous categories, contributing to a diverse range of solutions for urban aerial transport.</p></p>
